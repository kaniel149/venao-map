## NOTABLE PATTERNS & OBSERVATIONS

### Israeli Developer Community
A significant Israeli expat developer/investor presence in Playa Venao:
- Assaf & Liraz (El Sitio)
- Zeev Amir "Gigi" (Terranova) + Moledet Group (Israeli name)
- Tomer Joey Szuchman (Blue Venao, likely)
- Aviad & Omri (Green Ace)
- Selina founders (Rafael Museri & Daniel Rudasevski — global chain)

### AMF Capital Dominance
Scott Andrews / AMF Capital is by far the largest single landholder with 800+ acres, 3 residential developments, 2 hotels, 3+ restaurants/bars, a zipline adventure, and connections to the main school. This is essentially a company town for the non-beachfront portion.

### Market Dynamics
- Area described as "Panama's fastest-growing beach market" with 20-40% appreciation in 5 years
- Prices range from $195K (condos) to $1.5M (beachfront land)
- Strong short-term rental market ($1,800-2,400/month for Venao Park condos)
- 200+ families already purchased through AMF alone
- Multiple competing developers now active

### Panama Government Entities for Further Research
- **Registro Público de Panamá** (registropublico.gob.pa) — property title records
- **ANATI** (anati.gob.pa) — Autoridad Nacional de Tierras, land authority
- **Catastro** (catastro.mef.gob.pa) — cadastral/parcel office
- **Panadata** (panadata.net) — corporate registry search
- *Note: These databases require specific finca (property) numbers or company names. JS-heavy/login-required sites were not fully accessible via automated scraping.*
